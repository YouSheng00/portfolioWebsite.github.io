Cargo Loading Simulation

3D Interactive Simulation

This project, an interactive 3D simulation for the SATS Apron Ramp Training Department, aims to revolutionize training for new apron staff. Leveraging Unity Engine, I'm crafting immersive simulation experiences, supported by assets developed in Autodesk Maya and Adobe 3D Substance Painter. My main focus is on developing the 'Inflight Loading System,' a pivotal component of the Apron Ramp service. Specifically, I'm simulating the procedure for cargo staff to arrange diverse cargo pallets within the aircraft's cargo hold pre-departure. While the project is ongoing, I've completed approximately 70% of the process

Memories Flavour AR Project

Augmented Reality(AR) Project

In this project, our goal is to create an immersive AR experience that connects local food with the cherished memories of Singaporeans. With the slogan "Singapore Food & Memories Brewed," we have developed a series of postcards featuring three main themes: "Our Signature Food," "Makan mana (Where to eat)," and "吃饱没 (Eaten or not)." Each postcard corresponds to a specific theme in the AR experience. Users can scan these postcards with their mobile phones to explore various AR experiences. Our aim is to offer users a captivating blend of immersive memories, knowledge acquisition, and interactive AR games.

Dreaded Land VR

Virtual Reality(VR) Project

Dreaded Land is a virtual reality jungle survival game that was developed by me and my team using Meta Quest 2. Our aim with this project was to create a stimulating and realistic jungle survival simulation game using VR technologies. The game requires players to learn and experience jungle survival skills through the VR simulation experience. Our project showcases our knowledge and skills in Unity VR development, project management, 3D environment design, and UI/UX design.
